Group Investment Rounds

You are about to begin the group investment rounds. Partners will be randomly assigned and you will repeat this investment game in groups.

In these rounds:

  • You can invest up to $2 in increments of $0.5 in each round.
  • You will work with a partner to make investment decisions.
  • You can communicate with your partner only through the chat. External discussion is not allowed.
  • Your group will have a shared success number for each round.
  • Participants will equally share the profits or losses that your group achieves from the investment.
  • Use the slider to choose the investment amount after discussing it with your partner.
  • Once you have agreed on an investment amount, click the "Agree on Investment" button.
  • After one player "Agrees on Investment", the other player can finalize the investment amount.
  • Both partners must agree on the investment amount to continue.

Remember that effective communication and mutual agreement are key to successful group decisions.

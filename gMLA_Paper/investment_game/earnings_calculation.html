Earning Calculation

  • Successful investment: You earn 6 times the amount invested.
  • Failed investment: You lose the amount invested.
  • Money not invested in each round is added to your earnings.
  • Your earnings will be reported at the end of each round.
  • At the end of the individual part, your total result will be calculated.

For example,

  • Successful investment: You earn 6 times the amount invested.
    • Your "success number" was 4. Your die rolled a 4. This case is a "Success", you won. If you decided to invest $1 (which is within the $1 limit and in $0.25 increments), you will receive 1*6 = $6.

  • Failed investment: You pay the amount invested.
